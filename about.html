About Muslim Space

Building technology solutions for the Muslim community

Our Story

Muslim Space was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide free, accessible technology tools that empower Muslim organizations and communities to connect, share, and grow.

What started as a link shortener service has evolved into a comprehensive platform offering analytics, AI-powered tools, and community forms—all designed specifically to meet the unique needs of Muslim organizations.

Today, we serve thousands of community members and organizations across the globe, providing them with the same level of technology infrastructure that was previously only available to large corporations—all completely free.
