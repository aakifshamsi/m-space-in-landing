Frequently Asked Questions

How much time do I need to commit? ▼

We understand everyone has busy schedules. Even a few hours per week can make a significant impact. We work with your availability—no forced commitments.

Is this a paid position? ▼

This is a volunteer position—we're open-source focused. However, contributors get early access to new features, community recognition, and the satisfaction of helping thousands of people.

What kind of projects will I work on? ▼

We have various projects including our link shortener, analytics dashboard, events platform, and community tools. You'll have the opportunity to work on something that matches your interests and skills.

How do I get started? ▼

Fill out the form above! We'll review your application and reach out within a few days to discuss how you can best contribute.
