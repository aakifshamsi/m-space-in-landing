Frequently Asked Questions

How much time do I need to commit? ▼

We understand everyone has busy schedules. Even a few hours per week can make a significant impact. We work with your availability—no forced commitments.

Do I need to be Muslim to volunteer? ▼

Our primary mission serves the Muslim community, but we welcome volunteers of all backgrounds who share our values of community, privacy, and excellence.

Is this a paid position? ▼

This is a volunteer position—we're a non-profit. However, volunteers get early access to new features, community recognition, and the satisfaction of helping thousands of people.

How do I get started? ▼

Fill out the form above! We'll review your application and reach out within a few days to discuss how you can best contribute.
